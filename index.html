Our Story

Where Lagos Eats & Comes Alive

Speedy Dish Restaurant & Lounge was born from a simple idea: that great food and unforgettable nights belong together. Nestled in the heart of Ilupeju, we've been feeding Lagos with love since day one.

From our smoky suya grills to our sophisticated cocktail bar, every corner of Speedy Dish tells a story of culture, flavor, and energy. By day, we're your go-to for a quick, delicious meal. By night, we transform into Ilupeju's most vibrant lounge experience.
